Project

 

 

I am an aspiring Interior Designer from CEPT  University, Ahmedabad, India. I have been associated to Fablab CEPT for the past 4 years and have incorporated digital fabrication techniques for various academic projects.

I come from a country that is rooted in culture, hence I wish to develop gadgets that support and are a product of the fusion of digital technology and culture. Infusing the aesthetic sense of culture with present day's technology, I aim at developing a range of products that are a outcome of the both and also pursue my inclination towards digital fabrication as a medium of construction.

 

With Fab Academy, my aim is to understand and gain knowledge regarding 'making things' that can help me develop gadgets that support fusion of culture and technology.
